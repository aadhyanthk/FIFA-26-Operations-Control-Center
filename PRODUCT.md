# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
**Operations Control Center Staff** ("air traffic controllers" of the stadium).
Context: Working long shifts in dim rooms during high-stakes events (FIFA World Cup).
Job to be done: Monitor a 60,000-seat stadium, process incidents rapidly, and coordinate responses.

## Product Purpose
A digital twin of a World Cup stadium with an embedded AI operations agent that plans and executes workflows.

## Positioning
Transforms chaotic stadium incidents into precise, executable workflows while keeping humans in control.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, focused, dense, and enterprise-grade.

## Anti-references
- Consumer-grade, friendly "chatbot" interfaces.
- Bright/Light modes (causes eye strain in dim control rooms).
- Generic dashboards with low data density.

## Design Principles
- **Professional enterprise density**: Every pixel earns its place.
- **Instant readability**: Red/Amber/Green severity system ensures operators know where to look.
- **Human in control**: AI proposes the plan; the human approves.
- **Data in motion**: Real-time ticker-boards and causal updates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast typography against near-black backgrounds.
- Severity communicated through distinct shapes/badges.
